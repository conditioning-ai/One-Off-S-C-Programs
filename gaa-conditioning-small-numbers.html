Session Goal
Movement prep → max speed → constrained game load → aerobic-speed conditioning → game repeat → gym finish. Technical and physical stimulus in one block.
Format
With ball & without ball. Sessions can be run with or without ball — constraints and conditioning work remain the same either way.
Numbers
Designed for small groups. 3v3 +1 games require a minimum of 7 players. Scale constraints to group size.
Player Note
Sprint reps are max effort — rest is fixed. Game constraints change each game — read them before you start. Log gym weights.

Phase 01 · Warm Up
Movement
Preparation
~12 min
General Warm-Up
  • Jog 90 secs
Movement Circuit — continuous, no rest
  • Squats ▶ × 12
  • Inchworm to Push Up ▶ × 6
  • Forward–Reverse Lunges ▶ × 6 e/s
  • Bear Rolls ▶ × 6
Contact Activation
  • 1v1 — 1 Arm / 1 Leg Push Battle ▶ 30 secs e/s
📌 Pair up. Stay balanced on one leg, push opponent off balance using one arm. Builds stability and contact readiness.
Rugby Drill — With Ball
  • 5 Players jogging · 1 player weaves side to side to receive at each end 40–75m
⏱ Jogging pace throughout — this is activation, not conditioning. Rotate the weaving player each rep.
Movement circuit — quality over speed, full range on every rep
Push battle — stay tall, brace the core, don't grab
Rugby drill — passer leads, receiver times the weave to arrive on the ball
Phase 02 · Pitch
Max Speed
Sprint Work
~12 min
Sprint Ladder — Max Effort
  • 20m Sprint ▶ × 2 MAX
  • 30m Sprint ▶ × 2 MAX
  • 40m Sprint ▶ × 1 MAX
  • 50m Sprint ▶ × 1 MAX
⏱ 90 sec rest between every sprint — fixed, don't skip it
Every rep is max effort — if you can't give max, rest longer
Low start position on shorter reps, stand tall by 30–40m on longer reps
Drive arms aggressively — 90° at elbow, back pocket to cheek
Phase 03 · Pitch · With Ball
3v3 +1
Constrained Games
~12 min
Pitch Setup
  • Pitch dimensions45 × 30m
  • Work : Rest2 min on : 1 min off
  • Rounds× 4
  • ScoringPunched points or goals within 6m = 1 score
Game Constraints — 1 Per Game, Rotate Each Round
  • C1
    No hand pass over the head Invites better quality, lower passes and drives more purposeful hand passing angles
  • C2
    No 1-2's Removes the easy wall pass — invites 3rd man runs and deeper support patterns
  • C3
    10 seconds to score from possession Speeds up decision-making, reduces the influence of winning first possession, rewards quick transitions
  • C4
    +1 player is always on attacking team Numerical overload for the team in possession — encourages exploitation of space
🎯 Pick ONE constraint per game. Change it each round. Don't stack constraints — keep it clean so players can feel and adapt to each one.
2 min on is a HIGH intensity effort — treat it like a game
1 min off is full rest — use it, talk, reset
+1 player works hardest — rotate that role
Phase 04 · Pitch · No Ball
Intensive
Tempo Runs
~12 min
Main Work
  • 80m Intensive Tempo Run ▶ 2 sets × 3 reps
⏱ Recovery: walk back 40m between reps · Full rest between sets
Intensity Anchor
  • Effort level80–85% — not all out
  • All reps same paceFade = stop
These are aerobic-speed efforts — not sprints, not jogs
Consistent form across all reps is the goal
If pace drops significantly, stop the set there
Phase 05 · Pitch · With Ball
3v3 +1
Repeat Games
~12 min
Higher Density — Same Pitch
  • Work : Rest90 sec on : 30 sec off
  • Rounds× 6
  • Pitch & scoringSame as Phase 03
🎯 Same constraint system — pick one per game. The shorter rest period raises the aerobic demand significantly. Expect intensity to feel harder than Phase 03.
Without Ball Option
  • Replace game with shuttle runs inside the same pitch area 90 on : 30 off × 6
📌 Use this if ball isn't available or you want to isolate conditioning load from skill execution.
30 sec rest is short — stay on your feet, stay ready
By round 4–6 decision-making under fatigue is the key stimulus
Rotate the +1 player every 2 rounds
